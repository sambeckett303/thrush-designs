About Thrush Designs

Welcome to the heart of creativity, where artistry meets innovation. Born from a lifelong dedication to the craft, our artist's studio is a sanctuary of imagination and possibility. Here, ordinary items are elevated to new heights, transformed into captivating art pieces that defy convention.

But our artistry doesn't stop there. Step into the realm of garden design, where landscapes become living canvases. With a keen eye for detail and a love for nature's beauty, we craft breathtaking gardens that enchant and inspire.
